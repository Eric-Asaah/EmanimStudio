WHAT IS EMANIM STUDIO?
Emanim Studio is a comprehensive mathematical animation platform 
that enables educators, students, and content creators to produce 
professional mathematical visualizations with ease.

KEY FEATURES:
• Tons of Pre-built Mathematical Animation Templates
• Built-in Python, Manim & FFmpeg - No installations required
• Quality Settings: 480p, 720p, 1080p, 4K
• Real-time Rendering with Progress Tracking
• Self-Healing Diagnostics System
• Portable Design - Works on any Windows computer

QUICK START:
1. Launch Emanim Studio from Start Menu or Desktop
2. Select "Terminal Interface" for full control
3. Browse animations by category (Math, Physics, etc.)
4. Render professional videos in minutes
